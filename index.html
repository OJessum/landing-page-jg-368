Evolution

Beginning as a small but hostile facehugger, they will find a suitable host to implant an egg and produce a chestburster from within them.

Once the chestburster leaves the host, it'll begin to mature and form into a fully developed xenomorph with abilities similar to that of its original host.

While the types of Xenomorphs very in ferocity and strength, none are more dominant than the queen herself. Once she starts a hive of her own, there will be no other that can challenge her.
